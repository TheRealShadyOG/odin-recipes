Seared Ahi Tuna Steaks

Description

Seared ahi tuna steaks are very easy to make and takes only a few minutes to prepare. The best way to cook ahi tuna steaks is on the stove top in a pan, but you can also try and cook them on a grill, in the oven, or even an airfryer.

Pair this recipe with the buttered noodle recipe here for an easy and delicious meal!

Ingredients

  • 2 Ahi tuna steaks
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon whole peppercorns
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. Pat tuna steaks dry with papertowel. Season both sides with salt and cayenne pepper (Optional).
  2. Melt butter in a cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Add olive oil and pepper corns, cook until peppercorns soften and pop, about 5 minutes
  3. Gently place seasoned tuna steaks in skillet and cook until cooked enough to your liking, anywhere from 30 seconds to 1 1/2 minutes per side.
  4. Slice tuna into 1/4 inch slices (Optional) and serve.

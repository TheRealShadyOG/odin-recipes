Banana Bread Recipe

Description

Banana bread is really easy to bake and delicious. You can add in just about anything, from chocolate chips to chopped nuts. Its best to use bananas that are are over-ripe with browning skin and squishy inside. This recipe is for a 8 inch x 4 inch pan but you can use a different size pan (youll have to adjust the cooking time) if youd like.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Bananas
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the bananas with a fork until completely smooth. Stir in melted butter.
  3. Mix in the baking soda and salt. Stir in the sugar and beaten egg. Mix in the flour.
  4. Pour batter into the 8x4 pan
  5. Bake for 55 to 65 minutes at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). When timer ends insert a toothpick or wooden skewer into the center and if it comes out clean then it is done baking.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool in pan for a few minutes. Then remove the bread from the pan and let cool completely before serving.

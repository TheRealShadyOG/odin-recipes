Buttered Noodles

Description

This recipe is super simple, its just noodles with butter on them. It can be made as a main dish or as a side dish that you can pair with just about anything.

Ingredients

  • 1 Package of your favorite pasta
  • 4 tablespoons butter, cut into peices
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Grated or shredded cheese (Optional)

Steps

  1. Cook pasta as per instructions on package
  2. Mix butter, cheese (Optional), salt, and pepper into pasta until evenly combined.
